Mission

To create a dynamic platform that nurtures scientific curiosity, promotes innovative research, and bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications. We strive to inspire the next generation of scientists and technologists who will lead Bangladesh into a future of scientific excellence.

Vision

To establish ISTARC as South Asia's most prestigious science and technology festival, recognized globally for its contribution to scientific advancement and innovation. We envision a future where Bangladesh leads in scientific research and technological development, contributing significantly to global knowledge and human progress.

Our Values
